About Us

ITOTHYA General Agencies Ltd was created in 2005 and incorporated under the Companies Act in 2012. We have been providing professional services for over a decade, specializing in debt recovery, investigations, and consultancy. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure client satisfaction and provide effective solutions for businesses and individuals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to offer top-tier services in debt recovery, investigations, and asset management while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and transparency. We aim to support businesses and individuals by providing efficient and reliable solutions that help them achieve their financial and legal objectives.

Our Vision

We envision becoming a leading company recognized for its exceptional expertise in debt recovery and legal services. Our vision is to continue offering innovative solutions that cater to the ever-evolving needs of our clients, while maintaining our core values of integrity, transparency, and client satisfaction.

Our Services

  • Debt Recovery/Collection - We help businesses recover outstanding debts with a strategic and respectful approach, ensuring the protection of your reputation and minimizing legal risks.
  • Investigation/Skip Tracing - Our expert investigators use advanced tools and techniques to locate individuals or assets, helping clients recover what they are owed or resolve legal disputes efficiently.
  • Process Server - We ensure the proper delivery of legal documents, complying with local regulations and maintaining confidentiality throughout the process.
  • Asset Searches - We assist clients in locating assets, whether for legal purposes, financial planning, or debt recovery, by leveraging both traditional and cutting-edge research methods.
  • Debt Surveillance - Our team monitors debtors' activities to ensure that their financial obligations are being met, using legal and ethical practices to gather information as needed.
  • Consultancy - We offer expert consultancy in debt management, business process improvements, and legal strategies to help organizations navigate complex financial and legal issues effectively.
