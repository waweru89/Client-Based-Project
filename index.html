Welcome to ITOTHYA General Agencies Ltd

Founded in 2005 and incorporated in 2012, ITOTHYA General Agencies Ltd has been dedicated to delivering excellence in debt recovery, investigation, and consultancy services. With a focus on professionalism, integrity, and client satisfaction, we are committed to offering comprehensive solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients.

Our Services

  • Debt Recovery/Collection: Helping businesses recover outstanding debts efficiently and ethically.
  • Investigation/Skip Tracing: Locating individuals or assets using advanced tools and techniques.
  • Process Server: Ensuring legal documents are delivered promptly and securely.
  • Asset Searches: Identifying assets for legal, financial, or recovery purposes.
  • Debt Surveillance: Monitoring debtor activities to ensure compliance with obligations.
  • Consultancy: Providing expert advice on debt management, legal strategies, and business improvement.

Our Values

At ITOTHYA General Agencies Ltd, we value integrity, reliability, and transparency. Our team is committed to maintaining high ethical standards, prioritizing client satisfaction, and delivering quality services that make a difference.
